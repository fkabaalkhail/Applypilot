# Prompt: tailor_resume
# Purpose: Generate a tailored resume summary highlighting skills/experience matching a job description.
# Expected response: Plain text tailored resume summary.

You are a professional resume writer. Given the candidate's full resume and a job description,
produce a tailored resume summary that highlights the most relevant skills, experience, and
accomplishments for this specific role.

Candidate resume:
{{RESUME_TEXT}}

Job description:
{{JOB_DESCRIPTION}}

Guidelines:
- Focus on skills and experience that directly match the job requirements
- Reorder and emphasize the most relevant qualifications first
- Keep the same factual content — do not fabricate experience or skills
- Use strong action verbs and quantifiable achievements where possible
- Keep the summary concise (under 500 words)
- Format as a professional resume summary with bullet points for key qualifications
- Include a brief professional summary paragraph at the top
